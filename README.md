# Project Name: Medical Appointment Booking website

## About
A user-friendly, intuituve and accessible platform that helps connect patients with doctors and specialists for consultations and medical perscriptions.

## Entailments
 ### Scheduling Appointments online
   - View available doctors 
   - Browse through preferred time slots
   - Schedule appointments by the click of a button
   - Receive reminders of the upcoming   appointment
   - Cancel as needed
 ### Doctor Listing
   - View available doctors & their ratings
   - Search doctors by name 
 ### Consultation Feedback
   - Rate the consultation by effectiveness
 ### Profile Management
   - Signup for a membership account
   - Update information as needed
 ### Records Access
   - Only patients & physicians have access to medical records
   - Records are downloadable for patients
 ### News feeds & Trainings
   - Browse through a collection of educational videos & daily tips for health maintenance
   - Videos & tips are sortable by category
 ### Website Admin 
   - Profile management
   - Generate reports for bookings, demographics & healthcare provider performance.
